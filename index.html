Types of Coffee

Espresso – the base of all coffee drinks.
Doppio – a double shot of espresso, stronger.
Ristretto – the most concentrated espresso.
Americano – espresso diluted with hot water.
Cappuccino – espresso with milk and foam (1:1:1).
Latte – espresso with a lot of milk.
Flat White – a stronger latte, but without foam.
Macchiato – espresso with a drop of milk.
Mocha – espresso with chocolate and milk.
Affogato – ice cream topped with espresso.
Raf Coffee – espresso with cream and vanilla sugar.
Irish Coffee – coffee with whiskey and cream.
Turkish Coffee – coffee brewed in a cezve.
Vietnamese Coffee – strong coffee with condensed milk.
Nitro Coffee – cold coffee infused with nitrogen for a foamy texture.
